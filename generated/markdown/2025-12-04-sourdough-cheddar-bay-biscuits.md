# Sourdough Cheddar Bay Biscuits

#Sourdough #baking #biscuits #cheddar cheese #garlic

## Ingredients

- *1 cup* flour
- *1 teaspoon* salt
- *2 teaspoon* baking powder
- *0.75 teaspoon* garlic powder
- *0.667 cup, shredded* cheddar cheese
- *0.5 tablespoon, chopped* chives
- *0.5 tablespoon, chopped* parsley
- *0.5 cup, cold, cubed* butter
- *1 cup* sourdough discard
- *2 tablespoons* milk
- *0.667 cup, shredded* cheddar cheese
- *0.5 tablespoon, chopped* chives
- *0.5 tablespoon, chopped* parsley
- *1 cup* sourdough discard
- *2 tablespoons* milk
- *3 tablespoons, melted* butter
- *1-2 cloves, finely chopped* garlic cloves
- *0.5 tablespoon, chopped* parsley
- *0.5 tablespoon, chopped* chives

## Steps

1. In a small bowl, mix flour, salt, baking powder, garlic powder, cheddar
cheese, chives, parsley, butter, sourdough discard, milk. Set aside.

2. In a large bowl add the flour, salt, baking powder & garlic powder. Stir,
then add the cheese & fresh herbs: cheddar cheese, chives, parsley. Next add the
cold butter and crumble it into the dry ingredients until it resembles wet sand.
Add the liquid ingredients and mix until just combined: sourdough discard, milk.

3. Flatten on a floured surface into a 1 inch thick disk and cut your biscuits
into 6-8 pieces. Bake at 425Â°F for 20 minutes|25%minutes until golden brown.

4. Brush immediately with melted butter mixture: butter, garlic cloves, parsley,
chives.
