# Aperol Spritz Ice Cream

#recipe #ice cream #boozy desserts #summer desserts #orange flavor

## Ingredients

- *250 grams* mascarpone
- *250 grams* cream cheese
- *70 ml* fresh orange juice
- *50 ml* aperol
- *5 tbsp* honey
- *1 tsp* orange zest

## Cookware

- large bowl
- orange bowls

## Steps

1. In a large bowl, whisk together mascarpone, cream cheese, fresh orange juice,
aperol, honey, and orange zest until smooth.

2. Cover and chill the mixture for 60 minutes.

3. Churn in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturer, then transfer
to orange bowls and freeze until firm for 240 minutes. Garnish with fresh mint
leaves and serve.
