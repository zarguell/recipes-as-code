# Brown Sugar Espresso Overnight Oats

#coffee #overnight oats #recipe #breakfast #dalgona coffee

## Ingredients

- *1 cup* rolled oats
- *1 tbsp* chia seeds
- *0.25 tsp* salt
- *1 shot* espresso
- *1 cup* oat milk
- *1 tbsp* brown sugar
- *1 tsp* vanilla
- *0.5 cup* vanilla greek yogurt
- *1 tbsp* granulated sugar
- *1 tbsp* instant coffee
- *1 tbsp* boiling water

## Steps

1. Combine the overnight oats ingredients together in a bowl or jar, then cover
and place in the fridge overnight or for at least 4 hours. Add rolled oats, chia
seeds, salt, espresso, oat milk, brown sugar, vanilla, and vanilla greek yogurt.

2. In the morning, whisk the granulated sugar, instant coffee, and boiling water
together until it forms soft peaks. Add granulated sugar, instant coffee, and
boiling water.
