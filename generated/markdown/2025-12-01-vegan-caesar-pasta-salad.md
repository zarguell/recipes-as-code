# 2025-12-01-vegan-caesar-pasta-salad

#vegan #pasta #salad #plant-based #healthy

## Ingredients

- *2 cups* chickpeas
- *½ tsp* salt
- *1 tsp* smoked paprika
- *1 tbsp* olive oil
- *12 oz* pasta
- *4 cups* kale
- *1 cup* raw cashews
- *2 tbsp* lemon juice
- *1 tbsp* capers
- *1 tbsp* caper brine
- *2 tsp* dijon mustard
- *¾ cup* water
- *1 tsp* garlic powder
- *¼ tsp* black pepper
- *1 cup* vegan parmesan cheese

## Cookware

- oven
- baking sheet
- large pot
- large bowl

## Steps

1. >> Vegan Caesar Pasta Salad

2. Preheat your oven to 400°F. On a baking sheet, toss chickpeas with salt,
smoked paprika, and olive oil, then roast for 15 minutes until crisp.

3. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta until
al dente, 9 minutes. Drain. In a large bowl, add kale, washed and roughly
chopped; set aside.

4. Make the Caesar dressing: blend all dressing ingredients until smooth: raw
cashews, olive oil, lemon juice, capers, caper brine, dijon mustard, salt,
water, garlic powder, black pepper.

5. To assemble, add the cooked pasta to the bowl with kale; pour over the
Caesar dressing and toss to coat. Fold in vegan parmesan cheese, then top with
roasted chickpeas and toss gently to combine. Serve immediately or refrigerate
for later.
