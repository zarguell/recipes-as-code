# tiramisu brownies

#brownies #chocolate #tiramisu #dessert #coffee

## Ingredients

- *1 cup, 227 g* salted butter
- *14 oz, 70% cacao, coarsely chopped* bittersweet chocolate
- *1 cup, 60 g* unsweetened dutch cocoa powder
- *1 cup, 120 g* all-purpose flour
- *5 large* eggs
- *1 cup, 200 g* granulated sugar brownie
- *1 cup, 140 g* dark brown sugar brownie
- *1 tsp* brownie salt
- *1 tbsp* vanilla extract
- *1 tbsp, 30 ml* instant espresso powder brownie
- *8 oz* mascarpone
- *1 cup, 240 ml* heavy cream
- *0.25 cup, 50 g* granulated sugar cream
- *0.25 tsp* salt cream
- *1 tbsp* vanilla bean
- *1 tbsp, 30 ml* instant espresso powder cream
- *3 tbsp* unsweetened cocoa powder dusting

## Cookware

- oven
- baking dish

## Steps

1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F and line a baking dish with parchment paper.
In a heatproof bowl set over gentle simmer, melt salted butter together with
bittersweet chocolate until glossy and smooth. Whisk in unsweetened dutch cocoa
powder and all-purpose flour until just combined. Beat in eggs, granulated sugar
brownie, and dark brown sugar brownie until smooth. Stir in brownie salt and
vanilla extract and instant espresso powder brownie until fully incorporated.
Pour the batter into the prepared dish and bake for 25 minutes|30%minutes.
Transfer to a rack and let cool completely.

2. Meanwhile, in a bowl, beat mascarpone until smooth. In a separate bowl, whip
heavy cream with granulated sugar cream and salt cream until soft peaks form.
Beat in vanilla bean and instant espresso powder cream until incorporated.
Gently fold the espresso cream into the mascarpone until light and creamy.
Spread this over the cooled brownie layer, creating a generous, even layer.

3. Dust the top with unsweetened cocoa powder dusting and refrigerate the
assembled dessert for at least 2 hours|4%hours until set. Slice and serve.
